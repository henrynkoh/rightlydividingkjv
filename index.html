Core Method

Why Rightly Dividing?

Most Bible confusion comes from applying to one group what God said to another. Right division brings clarity.

📖

KJV Only

Every study uses the King James Version — preserved, precise, and the foundation of dispensational study.

✂️

Who? To Whom?

Before applying any verse, ask: Who is God speaking to? Israel, the Church, or the nations? Context determines application.

⏳

Dispensational

God dealt with mankind differently in different ages. Recognizing the current age of grace prevents misapplication of Kingdom or Law passages.

✉️

Follow Paul

For the Church age, Paul is our apostle. Romans through Philemon contains our doctrine, walk, and destiny as the Body of Christ.

🕊️

Grace Alone

Salvation in this age is by grace through faith — Ephesians 2:8-9. Not Law-keeping, not sacraments, not religious performance.

👑

Christ Is All

Every dispensation, every covenant, every shadow points to Him. Colossians 3:11 — Christ is all, and in all.
