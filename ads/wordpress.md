## Post body

> Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.  
> — **2 Timothy 2:15 (KJV)**

If you have ever felt lost jumping from Matthew to Romans to Revelation without a clear framework, you are not alone.

**Rightly Dividing KJV** is a free study series that walks through the King James Bible with one aim: help you *rightly divide* what God has written — so law is not confused with grace, and Israel’s program is not confused with the Body of Christ.
